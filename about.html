About us

CityTech Coding School offers a comprehensive curriculum that covers programming languages, web development, databases, software design, mobile application development, and data science, among other topics. Additionally, it imparts skills in software engineering principles, project management, and collaboration.

Our experienced instructors guide students through hands-on projects and practical exercises, allowing them to apply their knowledge and gain practical experience in web development. By the end of the program, students will have a solid understanding of web development principles and will be able to create dynamic and interactive websites and web applications.

What we do?

CityTech Coding School aims to prepare students for careers in the rapidly evolving field of web development. We foster a collaborative learning environment where students can engage with their peers, participate in coding challenges, and work on real-world projects. Join us at CityTech Coding School and embark on an exciting journey to become a skilled web developer.
